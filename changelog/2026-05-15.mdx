## Updates

### More reliable environment promotion from the AI agent

When you confirm a **Promote** action in the AI agent chat, your sandbox changes are now applied directly through the platform's promotion service instead of being routed back through the chat. You'll get a clearer success state, and failures surface immediately so you can retry without losing context.

No changes are needed on your side — promoting from sandbox to your non-sandbox environment works exactly as before. Read more about promoting between environments in the [BI Platform overview](/bi-platform/overview).
